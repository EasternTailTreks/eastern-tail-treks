About Eastern Tail Treks

We’re a friendly, local dog‑walking service dedicated to keeping Sydney’s pups happy and well‑exercised. Growing up with dogs of all sizes, we treat every pup like family and tailor each walk to their energy and comfort.

  • One‑on‑one care (or small group if requested)
  • Safe, familiar routes close to home
  • Photo & text update after every walk

FAQs

What do you need to know before a booking?

Age, breed, energy level, medical needs, behaviour on lead, and any quirks around dogs/people. Favourite treats or routes are a plus!

Where will you walk my dog?

Safe, local routes near your home and nearby parks suited to your dog’s comfort — e.g., Redfern Park, Moore Park, Centennial Park, and coastal paths near Bondi.

How long will you spend with my pet?

Bookings are for 30 or 60 minutes of walk time. Each includes a water break and a quick photo update afterwards.

Do you offer transport or training?

No car transport or formal training. We focus on safe, positive walks.

How do I book?

Click “Message on Facebook” above or send a DM via Messenger. We’ll confirm time, route, and any special needs.
